Game Rules

Objective: Achieve the highest combined financial growth metrics.

Number of Players: 2-6 players

Setup:

  • Each player starts with $1000 in MRR and ARR, 100 users, and 5% churn rate.
  • Players will take turns drawing and playing cards to increase their metrics.

Card Types:

  • Metric Cards: Increase MRR, users, or ARR with a detailed explanation.
  • Decision Cards: Make strategic decisions to grow your company with detailed impacts.
  • Event Cards: Respond to positive or negative events affecting your company with detailed impacts.

Game Length: The game lasts for 10 rounds.

Winning: After 10 rounds, the total annual revenue (ARR) and monthly revenue (MRR) are combined for each player. The player with the highest combined revenue wins the game. A celebratory message is displayed along with a summary of each company's metrics (total revenue, total customers, churn rate). Players can then choose to restart the game.
